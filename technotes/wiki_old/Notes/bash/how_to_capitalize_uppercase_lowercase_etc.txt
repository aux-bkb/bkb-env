====== how to capitalize uppercase lowercase etc ======
Created Wednesday 21 December 2016

Shell: How to make uppercase lowercase ...


echo "jsdfsj" | perl -ne 'print uc'

or

echo 'jsdf" | perl -pe '$_=uc'
