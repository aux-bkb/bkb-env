Getting Started

We have tried to make Notebooks as simple and intuitive as possible, but because of the considerable number of options we have prepared a Quickstart Guide, a Handbook as PDF and a Handbook as eBook. They will help you get started in no time. You can download each of them right away following these simple steps:

  • Tap the link above and Notebooks starts downloading the guide (you can tell by the spinning indicator in the status bar).
  • A few moments later you find the document at the top level of Notebooks. You can open and read the PDFs in Notebooks, but for reading the eBook, please choose your favorite eBook reader.
