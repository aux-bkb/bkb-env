Xterm: bigger fonts




xterm*font:     *-fixed-*-*-*-18-*

This informs xterm to use the 'fixed' font at size 18.

From here, you can either restart X or run 

   xrdb -merge ~/.Xresources 
   
in a terminal to update the settings
